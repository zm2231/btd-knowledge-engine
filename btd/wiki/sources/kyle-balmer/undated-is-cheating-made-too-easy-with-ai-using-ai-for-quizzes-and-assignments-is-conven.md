Short-form commentary on AI and academic integrity — acknowledging that AI makes cheating trivially easy while questioning whether the response should be detection tools or redesigning assessments entirely.

## Key Ideas
- AI makes quiz and assignment cheating trivially accessible for any student
- The real question is whether traditional assessment formats are worth defending
