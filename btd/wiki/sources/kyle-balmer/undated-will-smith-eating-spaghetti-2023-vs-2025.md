# Will Smith Eating Spaghetti: 2023 vs 2025

Side-by-side comparison of the infamous 2023 AI-generated video of Will Smith eating spaghetti (deeply glitchy) vs. a 2025 regeneration using current video models (dramatically more realistic). A clean, memorable benchmark of how fast AI video generation has improved.

## Key Ideas
- AI video quality has improved dramatically in two years — the 2023 benchmark looks absurd next to 2025 output
- Rate of improvement in AI video generation is faster than most observers expected
- This benchmark illustrates the pace-of-change pattern relevant to every AI domain
