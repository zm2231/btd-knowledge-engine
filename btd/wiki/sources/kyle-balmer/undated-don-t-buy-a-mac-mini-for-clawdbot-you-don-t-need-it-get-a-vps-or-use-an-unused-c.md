# Don't buy a Mac Mini for ClawdBot. You don't need it. Get a VPS or use an unused computer!

Short-form rebuttal to the advice that ClawdBot requires dedicated hardware. Balmer argues a cheap VPS or unused computer is sufficient for most personal use cases, saving hundreds of dollars on unnecessary hardware.

## Key Ideas
- A VPS ($5–10/mo) or any unused computer handles ClawdBot perfectly for personal workloads
- Mac Mini purchases for ClawdBot are unnecessary unless running GPU-heavy local models
- Infrastructure cost should not be a barrier to getting started with self-hosted AI agents
