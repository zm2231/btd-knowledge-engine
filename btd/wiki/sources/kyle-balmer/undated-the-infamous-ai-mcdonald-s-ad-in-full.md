# THE infamous AI McDonald's Ad in Full

45-second video showing the full AI-generated McDonald's advertisement that went viral for its uncanny and unsettling visuals. Balmer uses it as a cultural reference point for AI's current limitations in video generation.

## Key Ideas
- AI-generated video is commercially usable but still produces uncanny/glitchy artifacts at close inspection
- The ad went viral precisely because its imperfections are visible — a useful benchmark for where AI video stands
