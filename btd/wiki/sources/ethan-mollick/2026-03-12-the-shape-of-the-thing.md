# The Shape of the Thing

Mollick's synthesis of where AI has arrived and what comes next. The exponential gains across benchmarks — GPQA now at 94% vs. PhD-level 70%, GDPval parity with experts 82% of the time — are real and sustained. The most important new organizational experiment is StrongDM's Software Factory: a three-person team where AI agents write, test, and ship production code with no human touching the code. This points toward a new kind of work organization, not just faster individual productivity. The near-term environment will be unstable: AI capability crossing thresholds triggers sudden market reactions, organizational experiments, and policy conflicts — all simultaneously.

## Key Ideas
- We've moved from co-intelligence (prompting back-and-forth) to managing AI (assign work, get results, direct and evaluate)
- StrongDM's Software Factory is early evidence of a new organizational form — not just augmentation but restructuring
- Recursive self-improvement is on every major AI lab's roadmap: AI already partially writes the next generation of AI
- The window to shape how AI is used in organizations is open now — early adopters are setting precedents for everyone
- Instability is the defining feature of this moment; uncertainty is not the same as helplessness
