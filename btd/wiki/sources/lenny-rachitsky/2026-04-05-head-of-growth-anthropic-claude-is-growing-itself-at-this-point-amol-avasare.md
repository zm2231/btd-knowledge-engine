# Head of Growth (Anthropic): "Claude is growing itself at this point" | Amol Avasare

Amol Avasare (Head of Growth, Anthropic) breaks down how Anthropic scaled from $1B to $19B ARR in 14 months — the fastest growth trajectory in software history. Key themes: Claude is now writing its own growth experiment code via an internal tool called CASH; the team indexes 70/30 toward big bets (opposite of most growth teams); activation is the single highest-leverage growth problem in AI; and the PM-to-engineer ratio may need to invert as AI makes each engineer dramatically more productive. Amol's backstory — cold emailing the CPO with no job listing, overcoming a traumatic brain injury — is a case study in unconventional entry into a top role.

## Key Ideas
- Claude is now running its own growth experiments via Anthropic's internal "CASH" tool — AI managing its own growth
- 70/30 toward big bets vs. the typical 20/80 ratio — contrarian portfolio allocation produces outsized hits
- Activation (getting users to their first "aha" moment) is the highest-leverage growth lever, not acquisition
- PM-to-engineer ratio may invert as AI multiplies engineering output — fewer engineers, more strategic PMs
- Cold email with proof of work (a concrete, valuable insight) can get you into any room
