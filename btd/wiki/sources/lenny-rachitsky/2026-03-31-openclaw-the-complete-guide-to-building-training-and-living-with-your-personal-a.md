# OpenClaw: The complete guide to building, training, and living with your personal AI agent

Guest post by Claire Vo (CPO at LaunchDarkly, creator of ChatPRD) providing the definitive setup guide for OpenClaw — a personal AI agent platform Jensen Huang called "probably the single most important release of software, probably ever." The guide covers everything from first install to running a multi-agent team: choosing your infrastructure (Mac Mini, VPS, or hosted), training agents with your personal context, and practical workflows across email triage, calendar management, sales outreach, and family logistics. Claire went from public skeptic to running nine dedicated agents.

## Key Ideas
- Personal AI agents are now practical: the right setup can handle autonomous email, calendar, and communication tasks without supervision
- Agent specialization beats one-agent-does-everything: distinct agents for distinct roles (Polly for email, separate agents for sales, family, coding)
- Training agents on personal context (your writing style, your quirks, your priorities) is the leverage point
- Six ready-to-copy workflow templates: morning briefing, sales outreach, research, scheduling, family ops, coding
- The shift is from AI as tool to AI as team — the mental model change matters as much as the setup
