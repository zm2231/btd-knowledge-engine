# Quoting Giles Turnbull

A single sharp observation: everyone loves using AI to try someone else's profession, but resists when others use it for theirs. Captures the asymmetric threat perception that shapes how different professions are responding to AI — useful framing for understanding resistance to adoption.

## Key Ideas
- People evaluate AI displacement asymmetrically: fun to use in domains you don't depend on, threatening in domains you do
- This dynamic explains why professional resistance to AI varies so much by field — it's not about capability, it's about identity and economic stake
