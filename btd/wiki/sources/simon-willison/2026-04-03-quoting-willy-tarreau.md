# Quoting Willy Tarreau

HAProxy's lead developer documents a 5-10x spike in kernel security list reports since early 2026, with most now being correct — including duplicate findings from different AI tools hitting the same bug. Concrete data on how AI is flooding the security research pipeline.

## Key Ideas
- Volume went from 2-3 reports/week historically to 5-10 per day in 2026
- Duplicate reports are appearing — different people using different AI tools are finding the same bugs simultaneously
- Quality is high enough that additional maintainers had to be brought in to keep up
- AI-assisted security research is no longer a fringe concern; it's straining real infrastructure
