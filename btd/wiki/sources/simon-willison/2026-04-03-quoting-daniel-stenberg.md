# Quoting Daniel Stenberg

A short signal post: cURL's lead developer reports that AI-generated security reports have shifted from obvious slop to high-quality, real findings — and the volume is overwhelming his time. Willison collects this alongside similar reports from Linux kernel maintainers as evidence of a step-change in AI security research capability.

## Key Ideas
- The signal-to-noise ratio of AI-generated security reports has improved dramatically; the volume problem remains
- Open source maintainers are spending hours per day triaging AI-assisted vulnerability reports
- This is direct evidence the capability floor for AI security research rose sharply in early 2026
