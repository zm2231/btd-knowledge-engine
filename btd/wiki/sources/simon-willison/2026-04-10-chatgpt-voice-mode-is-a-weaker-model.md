# ChatGPT voice mode is a weaker model

Willison flags a non-obvious fact: ChatGPT voice mode runs on a GPT-4o era model with an April 2024 knowledge cutoff — far behind the frontier. Prompted by Karpathy's observation that AI capability perception varies wildly based on which access point and domain people encounter first, with voice being among the weakest.

## Key Ideas
- Voice mode's "smart AI assistant" UX creates false impressions of overall AI capability — it runs on a significantly older, weaker model than the text frontier
- Domains with verifiable reward functions (code, security research) have improved fastest because RL training is straightforward there
- The same provider can have a 1-hour autonomous code-restructuring agent and a voice assistant that fumbles basic questions simultaneously — these are very different products
- First impressions of AI capability are highly access-point-dependent; someone who only uses voice mode has a systematically outdated picture
