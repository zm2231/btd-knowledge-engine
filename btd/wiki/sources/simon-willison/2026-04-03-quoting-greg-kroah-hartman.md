# Quoting Greg Kroah-Hartman

Linux kernel maintainer Greg Kroah-Hartman describes a sharp transition: AI-generated security reports went from obvious junk to credible, real findings almost overnight in early 2026. One of the clearest first-person accounts of the step-change in AI security research quality.

## Key Ideas
- "Something happened a month ago, and the world switched" — the transition from AI slop to real security reports was sudden, not gradual
- All major open source projects are now receiving AI-assisted reports that are good and require genuine review
- Signals that frontier model improvements sometimes feel incremental but produce threshold effects in practice
