# Quoting Chengpeng Mou

OpenAI data point: ChatGPT is fielding ~2M weekly health insurance questions and ~600K weekly healthcare messages from people in "hospital deserts" — and 70% of those messages happen outside clinic hours. Illustrates LLMs filling real-world access gaps for non-technical users, relevant context for understanding who AI is actually serving.

## Key Ideas
- LLMs are functioning as primary healthcare information sources for underserved populations
- The usage pattern (outside clinic hours, remote areas) shows AI supplementing, not just replacing, existing access
- Usage at this scale signals broad deployment of LLMs in high-stakes information contexts
