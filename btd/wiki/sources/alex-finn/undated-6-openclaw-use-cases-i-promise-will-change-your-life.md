# 6 OpenClaw use cases I promise will change your life

Six concrete, high-value workflows Finn uses regularly with OpenClaw: research synthesis, document drafting, code review, competitive analysis, email triage, and a custom personal assistant setup. Each use case includes the exact prompt structure and any setup required. Practical for BTD builders looking for immediate workflow wins without building complex infrastructure.

## Key Ideas
- Research synthesis: dump a stack of links or documents, ask for structured summary — saves hours per week
- Document drafting: provide outline + context, let the agent draft, refine with targeted feedback
- Code review: paste code with specific concerns; agent identifies issues and explains tradeoffs
- Email triage: describe your decision criteria; agent categorizes and drafts responses by priority tier
- Personal assistant: CLAUDE.md with your preferences, calendar, and priorities turns it into a persistent assistant
