# Claude Mythos Changes Everything — Your AI Stack Isn't Ready

Claude Mythos is Anthropic's largest model release, and the bitter lesson applies: as models improve, complexity in your stack becomes a liability, not an asset. Nate's 4 audit questions (are you writing process specs or outcome specs? are your prompts brittle? are you routing tasks to the right model? are your evals testing the right thing?) help diagnose whether your current setup is ready for stronger models or will break under them.

## Key Ideas
- Bitter lesson: more capable models expose over-engineered stacks; simplify now
- Outcome specs outperform process specs as models get smarter — stop micro-managing steps
- 4 audit questions for stack readiness: outcome specs, brittleness, routing, evals
- Complexity accumulated for weaker models becomes drag with stronger ones
- Stack readiness is a strategic position, not a technical detail
