An AI-powered personal portfolio site functions as a queryable resume — recruiters or hiring managers can ask it questions and get answers grounded in your actual work history. The power dynamic inverts: instead of applying to hundreds of jobs, you become someone worth approaching. Built in Lovable with no code in a weekend. The underlying principle is showing over claiming: a fit assessment tool demonstrates confidence and capability simultaneously.

## Key Ideas

- Queryable portfolio flips the applicant-employer dynamic: you demonstrate judgment before the first call
- Fit assessment tool built in — lets you show you've thought about whether the role is right, not just whether you want it
- Lovable enables no-code builds; the skill is design and specification, not development
- Showing vs claiming: a working product says more than bullet points about skills
- Weekend timeframe is achievable; the barrier is deciding to build, not technical difficulty
