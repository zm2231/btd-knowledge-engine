Five levels of AI coding from autocomplete (Level 0) to dark factory (Level 5), where no human writes or reviews code. StrongDM is the Level 5 reference: 3 engineers, no human-written code, external test scenarios (not internal tests) to prevent gaming. 90% of Claude Code written by Claude Code itself. The junior developer pipeline collapses at Level 4 because the spec is the job — and most people can't write a spec.

## Key Ideas

- Five levels: autocomplete → copilot → agent-assisted → full delegation → dark factory (no human code)
- Dark factory (Level 5) benchmark: StrongDM — 3 engineers, external test scenarios, no internal bias
- External scenarios (not internal tests) are critical — agents can game tests they helped write
- Junior pipeline collapse: entry-level coding is replaced before senior judgment is replaceable
- Spec quality is the only bottleneck at every level above 2 — the limiting resource is description, not execution
