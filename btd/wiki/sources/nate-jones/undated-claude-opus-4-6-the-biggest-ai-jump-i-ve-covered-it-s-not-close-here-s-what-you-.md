# Claude Opus 4.6 — The Biggest AI Jump I've Covered. It's Not Close. Here's What You Need to Do.

The timeline of autonomous coding capability (30 minutes in early 2025 → 2 weeks by end of 2025) shows the pace of change. Rakuten managing 50 AI developers with one human illustrates the new organizational model. Nate argues this signals the arrival of "personal software" — software built by and for individuals at zero marginal cost.

- Timeline compression: 30 min → 2 week tasks in under 12 months
