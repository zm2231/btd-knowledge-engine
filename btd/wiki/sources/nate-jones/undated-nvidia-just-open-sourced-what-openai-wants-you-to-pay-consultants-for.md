# NVIDIA Just Open-Sourced What OpenAI Wants You to Pay Consultants For

NemoClaw open-sources the data pipeline infrastructure that enterprise AI consultants charge to build. Rob Pike's 5 programming rules applied to AI systems: good data engineering is good agentic engineering — the principles transfer directly. Enterprise AI adoption failures are almost always data problems disguised as model problems. The consulting industry's value proposition erodes as foundational infrastructure becomes free and open.

## Key Ideas
- NemoClaw: NVIDIA open-sourcing the enterprise AI data pipeline layer
- Rob Pike's 5 rules applied to AI: simplicity and clean data pipelines over clever architecture
- Enterprise AI failure pattern: complexity added where simplicity was needed
- Good data engineering = good agentic systems — the same discipline applies
- Open-source infrastructure commoditizes what consultants charge for, shifting value to judgment
