# Going Slower Feels Safer — But Your Domain Expertise Won't Save You Anymore. Here's What Will.

Two simultaneous collapses are underway: horizontal (every knowledge role converging toward AI orchestration) and temporal (the career leverage window compressing from years to now). Domain expertise still matters but no longer provides protection — the bike analogy captures it: knowing the destination doesn't help if you can't operate the vehicle. The meta-skill is driving AI agents, and "software-shaped intent" — the ability to translate goals into something machines can execute — is the new core competency.

## Key Ideas
- Horizontal collapse: all knowledge roles converging on AI orchestration as core function
- Temporal collapse: career leverage window compressing — the time to build is now, not later
- Domain expertise is necessary but no longer sufficient for protection
- Software-shaped intent: translating human goals into machine-executable specifications
- The meta-skill is agent operation, not any domain skill — this transfers across fields
