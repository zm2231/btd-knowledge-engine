# The Death of How-To Content

AI has commoditized procedural knowledge: any "how to do X" question gets a free, instant answer. This kills the economics of tutorial content and forces a shift in what creators and professionals must offer — not instructions but demonstrated process, evidence of taste, and proof of what's possible. The value is no longer in the answer; it's in watching someone navigate ambiguity.

## Key Ideas
- How-to content is losing value because AI gives the same answers for free
- What survives: showing your process, not just your output
- Demonstrating what's possible matters more than explaining how to do it
- The shift rewards taste and judgment over procedural expertise
- Builders who share their thinking in public create compounding value; those who only share finished outputs become interchangeable
