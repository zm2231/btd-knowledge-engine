# There Has Never Been More Opportunity Than Right Now

The opportunity isn't in bolting AI onto existing workflows — it's in rebuilding workflows from scratch around what AI can do. An SEO keyword research example shows the difference: using AI to do what you used to do faster versus using AI to do something that wasn't possible before. The METR study finding that experienced developers actually go slower with AI is used as evidence that the default posture (treating AI as an accelerant for existing habits) is the wrong frame.

## Key Ideas
- "Bolting on" AI to existing workflows captures a fraction of available leverage
- The real opportunity is rebuilding workflows around AI capabilities, not accelerating old ones
- METR study: experienced developers went slower with AI, suggesting their habits create drag
- The SEO keyword example shows the difference between using AI to do old work faster vs. enabling new work entirely
- Most people are optimizing within the wrong frame
