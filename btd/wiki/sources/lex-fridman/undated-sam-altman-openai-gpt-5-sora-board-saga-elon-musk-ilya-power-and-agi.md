# Sam Altman - OpenAI GPT-5 Sora Board Saga Elon Musk Ilya Power and AGI

Altman's second Lex appearance covers the November 2023 board crisis in detail — the most dramatic governance crisis in AI history — alongside GPT-5, Sora, and his predictions on compute as the defining resource of the decade. He frames the board saga not as a disruption but as a rehearsal for the much larger power struggles the industry should expect as capabilities scale toward AGI.

## Key Ideas
- The November 2023 OpenAI board saga was the most painful professional experience of Altman's life but a useful stress test of the organization's resilience
- Altman expected power struggles on the road to AGI — the board crisis came earlier than anticipated but confirmed the pattern
- Compute will be the most precious commodity of the coming decade; whoever controls it holds structural leverage
- GPT-5 and Sora represent the next capability tier — qualitatively different from GPT-4 in ways that matter for builders
- Trust in AI leadership requires earned legitimacy, not just technical competence — a theme the crisis forced into the open
