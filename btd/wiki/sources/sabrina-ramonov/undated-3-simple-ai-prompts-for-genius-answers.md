# 3 Simple AI Prompts for Genius Answers

Three one-liner prompt add-ons that dramatically improve LLM output quality — works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek. (1) "Ask me clarifying questions until you're 95% confident you can complete this task" forces the model to surface unknowns before acting. (2) "What would a top 0.1% person in this field think?" shifts the model into expert-evaluator mode. (3) "Reframe this in a way that challenges how I see the problem" breaks the model's tendency to agree and produce alternatives you hadn't considered.

## Key Ideas
- Appending clarifying-questions instruction prevents generic outputs and surfaces what you haven't thought about
- The top-0.1% framing triggers investor/expert level analysis — useful for business plans, strategies, pitches
- "Challenge how I see the problem" breaks sycophancy and forces genuine alternative framings
- These work as appended suffixes to any existing prompt — no restructuring needed
- Credit: the 0.1% tip originates from Ethan Mollick's context-engineering patterns
