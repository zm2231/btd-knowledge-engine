# The 8 AI Skills That Will Separate Winners From Losers in 2026

35-minute framework video from Ramonov laying out the 8 skills that differentiate top AI users from everyone else. The first three are foundational and non-AI-specific: (1) skepticism — develop immunity to hype and false income claims, (2) love learning — treat AI's fast pace as fun, not threatening, (3) learn in public — share what you learn before you feel expert. The next two are AI-specific: (4) context engineering over prompt engineering, (5) AI as sparring partner not answer machine. The final three are building skills: (6) vibe coding, (7) building AI systems, (8) documentation as the brain of your AI.

## Key Ideas
- Skepticism is skill #1: most AI income claims are exaggerated; cross-reference everything, including AI outputs
- Context engineering: provide role + situation + constraints + clarifying questions — don't obsess over prompt syntax
- AI sparring: use Claude to rip apart your strategy, find blind spots, and surface the questions you should have asked
- Vibe coding: describe your app in one sentence, start simple, iterate — no CS degree needed
- Documentation is infrastructure: quality of your AI systems equals quality of your documentation
