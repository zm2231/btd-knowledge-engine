# How To Start A 1-Person AI Business ($0 to $1M)

17-minute playbook from Ramonov (who built and sold an AI company for millions) on how to go from zero to $1M with one person in AI. The three-step framework: (1) lock into one AI niche for a full year, (2) build a personal brand by learning in public daily while in the learning phase, (3) launch a recurring-revenue school community teaching that skill. Emphasis on order of operations — most founders fail because they launch a product before building distribution.

## Key Ideas
- Lock in to one AI sub-niche for 12 months minimum — switching kills compounding
- Spend the first 3 months learning, not monetizing; build brand in parallel by posting daily "learn in public" content
- Information product recommendation: recurring community (e.g. School) over one-time courses for stable revenue
- Volume of content output matters most early — 5 posts/day across platforms gives fast feedback loops
- The formula: leverage = skill × clarity — document what your AI does so it runs without you
