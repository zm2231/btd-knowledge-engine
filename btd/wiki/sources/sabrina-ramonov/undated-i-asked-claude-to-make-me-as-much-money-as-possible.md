# I Asked Claude to Make Me as Much Money as Possible

Short-form experiment where Ramonov prompts Claude with an open-ended monetization goal and observes what it proposes. Commentary on what the experiment reveals about AI's reasoning around business strategy, what it gets right, and where it requires human judgment to be useful.

## Key Ideas
- Open-ended monetization prompts produce generic starting-point ideas — not personalized strategy
- Claude's value is as a sparring partner (refining your ideas) not an oracle generating ideas from scratch
- Combining context engineering (your situation + constraints) with this type of prompt produces actionable output
