# Stable diffusion dreams of psychedelic faces

A short (4-minute) generative art video — Karpathy running Stable Diffusion in a latent walk, producing looping psychedelic face imagery. No narration, no technical content. Documents an early-era exploration of diffusion model outputs as creative artifacts rather than functional tools.

## Key Ideas
- Latent space interpolation in diffusion models produces smooth, dreamlike transitions between images
- The outputs reveal what the model has internalized about human face structure at a perceptual level
- Early diffusion model artifacts (texture repetition, uncanny blending) are visible — a useful baseline for how far image generation has come
- Creative exploration is a legitimate mode of engagement with generative models, not just engineering
