# Lex Fridman

Long-form interviewer. His value in this corpus is not his own ideas but the conversations he draws out of guests who are hard to access elsewhere. Five episodes selected specifically for AI depth; these are the people building the systems everyone else talks about.

## Key Episodes

### Sam Altman x2
GPT-4 interview (2023) and GPT-5/Sora/Board Saga interview (2024). The first is Altman explaining what GPT-4 can and can't do. The second is Altman after the board coup, discussing power, governance, and where OpenAI is heading. Together they're the most comprehensive primary source on OpenAI's trajectory.

### Andrej Karpathy
Tesla AI, self-driving, Optimus, AGI. This is Karpathy in conversation rather than teaching mode; he talks about engineering judgment, building AI systems at scale, and what AGI might look like. Complements his YouTube tutorials (which are pedagogical) with his actual thinking about the field.

### Demis Hassabis
DeepMind, superintelligence, simulating reality. Different perspective from the OpenAI interviews; Hassabis comes from a scientific research background rather than a product/startup one.

### Yann LeCun
Meta AI, open source, limits of LLMs. The most contrarian voice in the corpus on AI; he argues current LLMs have fundamental limitations that scaling won't fix. Good counterpoint to Altman's optimism.

## When to Surface This Creator

- User wants to understand the AI industry landscape → Altman + Hassabis + LeCun give three different perspectives
- User is interested in Karpathy's thinking beyond tutorials → the Lex interview
- User asks about AGI, AI safety, or AI governance → these episodes cover it from practitioners, not commentators
